Ravelin - Capture The Flag Variant

Objective: Take your team's flag from
the spawn area of your base to the 
command center of the enemy base in
order to capture it.

Scoring: 10 points for each capture.

Other Notes: Use a detpack to destroy 
the enemy's power generator. This will
disable spot lights and laser security
grid guarding alternate entrance to the
command center. Engineer can repair the
power generator after it has been broken.
