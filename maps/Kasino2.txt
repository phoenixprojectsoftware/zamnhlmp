INSTALLATION:

1. 	Run Winrar and unzip kitkat_kasino2.rar

2. 	Open the kitkat_kasino2 folder and place the kitkat_kasino2.bsp, kitkat_kasino2.res, and kitkat_kasino2.txt in your valve/maps folder.  

3.	Place the 6 (six) city_nite.tga files into your valve/gfx/env folder.

4.	Put the kasino2 sound folder in the valve/sound folder.

5.	Place the kasino2 sprite folder in your valve/sprites folder.

6.	Place the kasino2 models folder inside the valve/models folder.

7.	Place the special player models in the valve/models/player folder.
