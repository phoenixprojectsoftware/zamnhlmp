INSTALLATION:

1. 	Run Winzip and unzip kitkat_kasino.zip.

2. 	Open the kitkat_kasino folder and place the kitkat_kasino.bsp, 				kitkat_kasino.res, and kitkat_kasino.txt in your valve/maps folder.  

3.	Place the 6 (six) chaoscity.tga files into your valve/gfx/env folder.

4.	Place the wav files in a folder called casino and place that in your valve/sound 		folder.

5.	Place the models in a folder called casino and put that in your valve/models
	folder.

6. 	Put the angelsmoke folder in the valve/models/player folder.
